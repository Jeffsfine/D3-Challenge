American Health Risks Dashboard -

An interactive visual of trends of health factors across the country.

Click on any of the axes to visualize various trends.
Hover over any state to see its' individual standing for each category for your selected view.

The relationships plotted are those of state-based percentage of population who:

  • smoke, are obese, lack healthcare, or are considered to be living at or under the poverty line,
And also state-based
  • median age and household income.
Each state's Median age, household income, and poverty rates are plotted against the smoking, lack of healthcare, and obesity rates.

Key Findings:

  • Median household income correlates negatively with each of the risk factors (Obesity, Smoking, and Lack of health care)
  • Poverty % correlates positvely with each of the risk factors
  • Median Age is heavily concentrated around 30-45 years
